You may assume no duplicates in the array.

Example 1:
Input: [1,3,5,6], 5
Output: 2

Example 2:
Input: [1,3,5,6], 2
Output: 1

Example 3:
Input: [1,3,5,6], 7

Input: [1,3,5,6], 0
